How to use?

How to Start?

You can open the search bar with AltS (by default).

You can open the settings popup with AltShiftS (by default).

You can change the shortcut settings here:

chrome://extensions/shortcuts

Other key actions

  • /

    • Focus on search bar (when the search bar is displayed).

  • Esc

    • Unfocus on search bar (if you are currently focused on search bar).

  • ↑↓

    • Switch search engines (if you are currently focused on search bar).

  • Enter

    • Search (if you are currently focused on search bar).

Anywhere Search popup

What is 'Search bar position'?

You can choose your search bar position from the following four positions:

Top Left

Top Right

Bottom Left

Bottom Right

What is 'Theme'?

You can choose your search bar theme from the following two themes:

Light

Dark

What is 'Search results display format'?

  • Open in new tab (Active)

    • Default. Open the search results in a new tab and move on.

  • Open in new tab (Not active)

    • Opens the search results in a new tab, but does not move.

  • Open in current tab

    • Opens the search results in the current tab.
